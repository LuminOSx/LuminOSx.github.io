Features

Luminus is an accessible and open source operating system suitable for beginners and experienced computer users.

Focused on productivity

Offering all the advantages of cutting-edge software combined with an emphasis on immediate start, out-of-the-box automated configurations to require less manual intercession and readily accessible applications when needed.

Visual integrity between applications

Integrating quality and visual cohesion between all applications in the system using KDE Plasma, thus making a more connected environment and delivering a unique visual identity between the system.

Balance performance and safety

Whenever possible, we offer the best performance and security configuration for the system as a whole and for individual applications, bringing greater quality and security.

Open community

We are continually building an open community, so we are fully willing to listen to suggestions and criticisms to further improve the ecosystem of LuminOS and the community itself.
